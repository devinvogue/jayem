About KETSA

The Keep The Smiles Alive (KETSA) community is a humanitarian organization based in Ghana. We seek to provide inspiration to the youth in our various communities. Hence, we are an inspirational brand that believes in the smiles of everyone. At KETSA, we believe a smile is the language even a baby understands and though it happens in a flash, the memory it leaves is a lifetime. And so, we are encouraged to leave long lasting memories in the hearts and faces of the youth we encounter. We know that if we can smile and cause others to smile too, then the world will be a better place. At KETSA, we believe a smile must come from within. This means, one must be inspired and convinced enough to allow him or herself be happy and SMILE! Hence, we encourage the youth with words. We do this through our various activities which include ‘Picquotes’ and Seminars. Also, we do charity works to encourage the youth to always give back to society.

Mission

To bring inspiration and rekindle hope in every youth through long lasting memories.

Vision

To be inspired to inspire

OBJECTIVES

To rekindle hope in every youth (so that they value life) through mentorship.

To remind every youth of the realities of life and good morals through mentorship in life choices.

To create long lasting memories for the youth through diverse ways.

To empower the youth to take up responsibilities and work hard towards their dreams through mentorship in career.

To educate the youth on decision making.

To provide financial education for the youth.

To share life experiences with the youth (so that they are encouraged knowing that they are not in it alone).

Our Work!

Donations

We donate items to communities and charity homes as a symbol of encouraging the youth to give back to society. You can also support what we do by donating both in cash and kind towards our various activities. Depending on the items we are donating for a particular outreach, you can donate in kind. Cash donations are always welcome. You can send your cash donations to our Bank Account or Mobile money account.



Volunteering

You can become a part of the KETSA community by joining us for our various charity outreaches as well as seminars for the youth. Contact us if you are interested.



Picquotes

‘Picquotes’ is the name we give to our inspirational pictures. It is an inspirational quote with a picture of the brain behind the quote at the background (a smiling picture). You can be a part of this by sending a ‘smiley’ picture of yourself plus your personal quote to our email; ketsagh@gmail.com and we shall feature you on our pages. Please note that the quote should be your own quote and not copied. The quote should be anything about life that keeps you going. Be encouraged to join us in this because you never know who will be inspired by your words.



Seminars

The main activity for KETSA Community is to organize seminars for the youth, where resource persons educate the youth on the various aspects of life and growth in the society.

Share your story

You can again be a part of what we do by sharing your story to inspire others. You can send us your life experiences; the ones that made you stronger and taught you never to give up in life, and we will upload them on our website to encourage more people. You can send your story to our email; ketsagh@gmail.com.
