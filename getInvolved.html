HOW TO GET INVOLVED!

Volunteering

You can become a part of the KETSA community by joining us for our various charity outreaches as well as seminars for the youth. Contact us if you are interested.

Donations

You can also support what we do by donating both in cash and kind towards our various activities. Depending on the items we are donating for a particular outreach, you can donate in kind. Cash donations are always welcome. You can send your cash donations to our Bank Account or Mobile money account. Bank Account: Mobile Money Account: **(I will send details of the account numbers later; I'm working on them)**

Share your story
You can again be a part of what we do by sharing your story to inspire others. You can send us your life experiences; the ones that made you stronger and taught you never to give up in life, and we will upload them on our website to encourage more people. You can send your story to our email; ketsagh@gmail.com.

Picquotes

‘Picquotes’ is the name we give to our inspirational pictures. It is an inspirational quote with a picture of the brain behind the quote at the background (a smiling picture). You can be a part of this by sending a ‘smiley’ picture of yourself plus your personal quote to our email; ketsagh@gmail.com and we shall feature you on our pages. Please note that the quote should be your own quote and not copied. The quote should be anything about life that keeps you going. Be encouraged to join us in this because you never know who will be inspired by your words

Our Work

Seminars

The main activity for KETSA Community is to organize seminars for the youth, where resource persons educate the youth on the various aspects of life and growth in the society.

Charity

Another activity of the KETSA community is charity work. We donate items to communities and charity homes as a symbol of encouraging the youth to give back to society.

Picquotes

In addition to our activities, we also design ‘picquotes’ which is a quote with a picture of the brain behind the quote at the background. We feature these pictures on our various pages, that is Facebook, Twitter and Instagram. This is our way of inspiring more people on social media.
