ABOUT US

Discover us!

KETSA stands for: Keep The Smiles Alive. The Keep The Smiles Alive (KETSA) community is a humanitarian organization based in Ghana. We seek to provide inspiration to the youth in our various communities. Hence, we are an inspirational brand that believes in the smiles of everyone. At KETSA, we believe a smile is the language even a baby understands and though it happens in a flash, the memory it leaves is a lifetime. And so, we are encouraged to leave long lasting memories in the hearts and faces of the youth we encounter. We know that if we can smile and cause others to smile too, then the world will be a better place. At KETSA, we believe a smile must come from within.

This means, one must be inspired and convinced enough to allow him or herself be happy and SMILE! Hence, we encourage the youth with words. We do this through our various activities which include ‘Picquotes’ and Seminars. Also, we do charity works to encourage the youth to always give back to society.

Vision

To be inspired to inspire.

Mission

To bring inspiration and rekindle hope in every youth through long lasting memories.
