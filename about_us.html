ABOUT US!

KETSA stands for: Keep The Smiles Alive. The Keep The Smiles Alive (KETSA) community is a humanitarian organization based in Ghana. We seek to provide inspiration to the youth in our various communities. Hence, we are an inspirational brand that believes in the smiles of everyone. At KETSA, we believe a smile is the language even a baby understands and though it happens in a flash, the memory it leaves is a lifetime. And so, we are encouraged to leave long lasting memories in the hearts and faces of the youth we encounter. We know that if we can smile and cause others to smile too, then the world will be a better place. At KETSA, we believe a smile must come from within. This means, one must be inspired and convinced enough to allow him or herself be happy and SMILE! Hence, we encourage the youth with words. We do this through our various activities which include ‘Picquotes’ and Seminars. Also, we do charity works to encourage the youth to always give back to society.

Vision

To be inspired to inspire

Mission

To bring inspiration and rekindle hope in every youth through long lasting memories

Brand texts

Hebrews 3:13‘But encourage one another daily, as long as it is called Today……’ NIV

Romans 12:8‘If it is to encourage, then give encouragement……’ NIV

Objectives

  • To rekindle hope in every youth (so that they value life).
  • To remind every youth of the realities of life and good morals.
  • To create long lasting memories for the youth through diverse ways.
  • To empower the youth to take up responsibilities and work hard towards their dreams.
  • To educate the youth on decision making.
  • To provide financial education for the youth.
  • To share life experiences with the youth (so that they are encouraged knowing that they are not in it alone).
